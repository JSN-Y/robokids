# 🤖 Robokids

Welcome to **Robokids**, an interactive and creative game designed specifically for kids! Dive into exciting adventures, defeat bosses, and unleash your imagination by building your very own custom levels.

## ✨ Features

### 🎮 Player Experience
* **Epic Boss Battles:** Fight and defeat challenging bosses to clear stages and prove your skills.
* **Level Progression:** Play through a variety of fun, built-in levels to advance in the game.
* **Custom Level Creator:** Unleash your creativity! Use the built-in editor to design, build, and play your own personalized levels.
* **In-Game Economy:** Earn coins by defeating enemies and successfully clearing levels.
* **Item Shop:** Spend your hard-earned coins in the shop to unlock cool new skins and powerful weapons.

### 🛡️ Administrator Tools
* **User Management:** Seamlessly create and manage new user accounts.
* **Stat Modification:** Modify player resources on the fly, including adjusting coin balances and adding or removing hearts (health/lives) for specific users.
